About SF Peace Parks

SF Peace Parks is targeted to help youth, from ages 16 to 25. The program is a collaboration between youth residents, the San Francisco Police Department, the Department of Public Health and the Recreation & Park Department. These collaborations allow opportunites foryouth residents in San Francisco to build healthy relationships with local law enforcement members to help create a sense of safety and community.
